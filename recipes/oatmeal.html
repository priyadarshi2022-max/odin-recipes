Oatmeal Recipe

Home

Oatmeal is a preparation of oats that have been dehusked, steamed, and flattened, or a coarse flour of hulled oat grains that have either been milled, rolled, or steel-cut. Ground oats are also called white oats. Steel-cut oats are known as coarse oatmeal, Irish oatmeal, or pinhead oats.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup of almond milk
  2. 3 spoons of oats
  3. 3 spoons of yogurt

Toppings

  1. Blueberries
  2. Banana
  3. Almonds
  4. Raisins
  5. Nuts
  6. Vanilla extract
  7. Apple

Method

Step 1

Mix oats with milk and yogurt

Step 2

Add your bananas, blueberries and almonds (or any or very nuts of your choice)

Step 3

Cover the lid of the container

Step 4

Leave it all night in the refrigerator

Eat this delicious meal at breakfast
